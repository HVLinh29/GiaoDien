FACTORY OVERVIEW

Lumi Smart Factory specializes in the manufacture of smart home and IoT devices… Leveraging its advanced production lines, cutting-edge manufacturing technologies, and highly qualified workforce, Lumi Smart Factory expands its services to include:

  • OEM
  • ODM

SERVICES

ODM (Original Design Manufacturer)

With our cutting-edge machinery and professional engineers, the factory is available for consultation and support in designing original components and products.

OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer)

We manufacture electronic devices for any order, based on pre-existing designs, focus on delivering high-quality products within the specified timeframes.

EMS (Electronic Manufacturing Services)

We offers comprehensive electronic manufacturing services, including design and production of electronic devices for both domestic and international partners.

ADVANTAGES & SUPERIOR CAPABILITIES

High-tech factory

Investment in advanced and cutting-edge production lines and machinery: Yamaha-JP’s Printer/SPI, Mounter, and AOI; Heller-USA’s Reflow; Eflex-HK’s Conveyor, Loader, and Unloader; and other components and equipment from reputable suppliers in the USA, Japan, and Korea.

The entire factory is equipped with comprehensive smart security and smart lighting solutions.

Professional production management

All production data is stored in an internal data management system in order to evaluate and improve production quality over time.

Strict quality control

The output products fulfill ISO 9001:2015 quality standards and have a reliability level with an AQL (Acceptable Quality Level) of less than 0.1%

Comprehensive consulting and design services

Lumi Smart Factory offers comprehensive consulting and design services for all industries and products. The factory has successfully consulted and designed for many domestic and international partners, such as Etec – Israel, Hogar Control India, Eureka India, and Elite – Australia.

Highly skilled workforce

Lumi Smart Factory owns a team of professional electrical and automation engineers with over 5 years of experience in operating production lines and has hold many patents in the field of electronics and information technology.

Diverse supply chain

The factory has a diverse supply chain, including suppliers of electronic components, mechanical materials, packaging materials, etc., both domestically and internationally. This ensures a steady availability of raw materials.
